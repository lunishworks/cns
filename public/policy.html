0. Identity and contact details

CNS Studios is a Project of me, Aaron Gerkens. I maintaine all these services running on the `cns-studios.com` domain and all subdomains. If you want to contact me: My Full Government name: Aaron Gerkens My Email Adress: aaron.gerkens@proton.me

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

CNS Studios collects minimal information necessary to provide our services. We use this data to keep our systems secure. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining data security. All Information is used to prevent spam, cyper attacks and save data for Users using our Services.

1.1 Account Information

  • Username (chosen by you). This is used to verify the Identity of the user in association with the PIN. Without these, we cant grant access to the Users data, delete or refactor them. If a User forgets their credentials, we have to verify with other metrics such as login behaviour or IP Locations.
  • 4-digit PIN (encrypted). This is savely stored on our servers and encrypted. We use this PIN to verify the Users Identity. Its encrypted and can only be encrypted with the matching JSON Webtoken String. This String is stored on our Servers and without it, PINs cant be dencrypted. The owner, Aaron Gerkens, can still delete the PIN if the JSON Webtoken String is gone
  • Last login IP Adress. This is used to prevent Spam and detect other unusual activitys, so we can detect Cyber attacks. This information is stored for 7 Days after creation and is then deleted
  • Last login timestamp. Thisi is used to prevent Spam and detect other unusual activitys, se we can detect and prevent Cyber attacks.

1.2 Usage Data

  • Project interactions. Without it, Users will lose any progress they made on any of our Services.

1.3 Cookies

  • Authentication tokens (httpOnly cookies). Without it, Users would have to log in every time they load any of our sites. THe Authentication tokens are stored 7 Days in the Browser Cache of the User and are then expired, and cant be reused.
  • User preferences. Such as Light/Dark mode or Audio on/off. These informations are only stored in the Users Browser Cache.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Account authentication and security
  • Providing access to CNS Studios services
  • Maintaining system security

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • PINs are encrypted using bcrypt (10 salt rounds)
  • Secure httpOnly cookies prevent XSS attacks
  • SQLite database with encrypted storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No plain-text password storage

4. DATA SHARING

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data remains within CNS Studios systems and is never transferred to external entities, except when required by law.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Modify your account information
  • Delete your account information and all Data associated with it

To exercise these rights, contact us at aaron.gerkens@proton.me or log into the main site `cns-studios.com` and navigate to account.

6. COOKIES POLICY

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Authentication and session management
  • Duration: 1 hour to 7 days (based on checking the Box "Remember Me" on the Login Page)
  • Type: httpOnly, secure, SameSite cookies

You can clear cookies at any time by clearing browser data.

7. DATA RETENTION

Account data is retained while your account is active. Inactive accounts may be archived after 2 years of inactivity. You can request immediate deletion at any time.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

CNS Studios services are not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
