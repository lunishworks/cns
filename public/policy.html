LAST UPDATED: DECEMBER 13th, 2025

0. Identity and contact details

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

CNS Studios collects the minimal information necessary to provide our services. We use this data to keep our systems secure. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining data security. All information is used to prevent spam, cyberattacks, and to save data for users of our services.

1.1 Account Information

  • Username (chosen by you). This is used to verify the identity of the user in association with the PIN. Without these, we can't grant access to the user's data, delete, or modify it. If a user forgets their credentials, we have to verify their identity with other metrics such as login behavior or IP locations.
  • 4-digit PIN (encrypted). This is safely stored on our servers and encrypted. We use this PIN to verify the user's identity. It's encrypted and can only be decrypted with the matching JSON Web Token string. This string is stored on our servers, and without it, PINs can't be decrypted. The owner, Aaron Gerkens, can still delete the PIN if the JSON Web Token string is gone.
  • Last login IP address. This is used to prevent spam and detect other unusual activities, so we can detect cyberattacks. This information is stored for seven days and is then deleted.
  • Last login timestamp. This is used to prevent spam and detect other unusual activities, so we can detect and prevent cyberattacks.

1.2 Usage Data

  • Project interactions. Without it, users will lose any progress they made on any of our services.

1.3 Cookies

  • Authentication tokens (httpOnly cookies). Without them, users would have to log in every time they load any of our sites. The authentication tokens are stored for seven days in the user's browser cache and then expire, and can't be reused.
  • User preferences, such as Light/Dark mode or Audio on/off. This information is only stored in the user's browser cache.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Account authentication and security
  • Providing access to CNS Studios services
  • Maintaining system security

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • PINs are encrypted using bcrypt (10 salt rounds)
  • Secure httpOnly cookies prevent XSS attacks
  • SQLite database with encrypted storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No plain-text password storage

4. DATA SHARING

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data remains within CNS Studios systems and is never transferred to external entities, except when required by law.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Modify your account information
  • Delete your account information and all data associated with it

To exercise these rights, contact us at aaron.gerkens@proton.me or log into the main site `cns-studios.com` and navigate to your account.

6. COOKIES POLICY

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Authentication and session management
  • Duration: 1 hour to 7 days (based on checking the "Remember Me" box on the login page)
  • Type: httpOnly, secure, SameSite cookies

You can clear cookies at any time by clearing your browser data.

7. DATA RETENTION

Account data is retained while your account is active. Inactive accounts may be archived after 2 years of inactivity. You can request immediate deletion at any time.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

CNS Studios services are not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or system notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
