FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: HOW DO I RESET MY PIN?
Contact support at support@cns.studio with your username. We'll verify your identity and help you reset your PIN securely.
Q: WHY ISN'T MY LOGIN WORKING?
Ensure you're entering the correct 4-digit PIN. Usernames are case-sensitive and PINs must be exactly 4 numbers. Clear your browser cookies and try again.
Q: HOW DO I DELETE MY ACCOUNT?
Email privacy@cns.studio with your username and request account deletion. We'll process your request within 48 hours.
Q: IS MY DATA SECURE?
Yes! We use bcrypt encryption for PINs, httpOnly cookies, and secure database storage. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: IS EVERY PROJECT OPEN SOURCE?
Yes! You can find all our private and public projects on our GitHub. Feel free to edit and contribute.
Q: WHERE CAN I REPORT BUGS?
Report bugs via GitHub Issues or email support@cns.studio with detailed reproduction steps, browser info, and screenshots if applicable.
